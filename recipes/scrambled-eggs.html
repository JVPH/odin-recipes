Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Classic Scrambled Eggs

With this 5-ingredient recipe, the scrambled eggs come out soft, creamy, and flavorful every time. An easy, delicious breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Extra-virgin Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a medium bowl and add the milk. Whisk until smooth and combined
  2. Brush a small nonstick skillet with olive oil and bring to medium heat
  3. Pour in the eggs. Pull a rubber spatula across the bottom of the pan to form large, soft curds of scrambled eggs.
  4. Continue cooking over medium-low heat, folding and stirring the eggs every few seconds
  5. Remove the pan from the heat when the eggs are mostly set. Season to taste with salt and pepper and garnish with chopped fresh chives, if desired
