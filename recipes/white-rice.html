White Rice in a Pot

White Rice Recipe

Simple and tasty white rice recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of White Rice
  • 1 and 1/2 Cups of Water
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. In a medium size pot, add rice, water and salt. Set over medium-high heat and bring to a boil
  2. Lower to a simmer and cover. Lightly simmer for 15 minutes
  3. Remove from heat and steam an additional 15 minutes
  4. Place on a pan to cool
